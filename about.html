WELCOME TO RAIPUR CITY

Raipur City website is your go-to source for everything related to Raipur, from the latest news and events to historical insights and cultural highlights. Our mission is to provide comprehensive and up-to-date information to residents and visitors alike.

OUR HISTORY

Founded in the 9th century, Raipur has deep historical roots, serving as the capital of the Haihaya kings. Over centuries, Raipur has transformed into a major hub in central India, evolving from its ancient past to become a key center for industry, commerce, and education.

OUR VISION

Raipur strives to be a smart, sustainable city that offers its residents a high quality of life. Our goal is to foster inclusive growth, drive innovation, and create a city that thrives economically while preserving its cultural and natural heritage.
